# Zorn Showcase Scenario Catalog

The showcase scenario catalog defines planned demos and validation bundles.
Scenario files live under `examples/scenarios/`; the canonical registry lives in
`docs/manifests/zorn-showcase-scenarios.json`.

## Planned Scenarios

- SCN-002: Maritime AIS Tracking and Media.
- SCN-003: DIS Multi-Domain Replay.

- SCN-005: Mission Autonomy Patrol.
- SCN-006: Edge Mesh Partition and Reconnect.
- SCN-007: Partner Integration Fair.
- SCN-008: Space Track Awareness Mock.
- SCN-009: Undersea Sparse-Comms Search.
- SCN-010: Readiness and Pre-Launch Checklist.
- SCN-011: Full Evaluation Bundle.

## Safety And Boundary Notes

Domain scenarios are synthetic and non-operational. They must not implement real
fire-control, engagement recommendations, targeting, weapon release, or
operational tactics. They should produce believable Entity/Task/Object behavior
for testing, demos, and local development only.
